About

I'm a 4th-year Integrated M.Tech student in Information Technology at IIPS, DAVV, and I spend most of my time on a single question: how do AI systems fail, and how do we make them reliable?

My research so far has centered on adversarial robustness and the security of vision-language models and deep neural networks — work I've been fortunate to pursue at IISER Bhopal and IIT Bhubaneswar. That's my current home base, but not my ceiling.
